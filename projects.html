Autonomous Drone build

Status: In-Progress (Not Functional)

This is my first attempt at building a drone from the ground up. For now it utilizes a PX4IMUv1 and I'll hopefully be able to interface it with a raspberry pi running ROS. So far it's been difficult to work with due to the extreme obscurity of the PX4IMUv1, I might just upgrade to a legitimate PixHawk when funds allow due to availability of documentation.

Drone Tracking and Homing

Status: In-Progress (Partial Functionality)

My use of OpenCV and ROS. The project aims to use the monocular camera to control the movements of a AR.Drone 2.0. It does this by tracking a color and keeping it within view using control algorithms. The idea is to eventually make this turn into a drone follower.
